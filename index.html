Monkey D. Luffy

Strengths: Strong willpower, rubber body, skilled in Haki.

Weaknesses: Weak against sharp objects, reckless at times.

Best Moves: Gum-Gum Pistol, Gum-Gum Bazooka, Gear Fifth

Roronoa Zoro

Strengths: Master swordsman, incredible endurance, strong Haki.

Weaknesses: Poor navigation skills, stubbornness.

Best Moves: Three-Sword Style, Ashura, Onigiri.

Nami

Strengths: Excellent navigator, skilled in weather manipulation.

Weaknesses: Physical strength is not her strong suit.

Best Moves: Thunderbolt Tempo, Clima-Tact Techniques.

Usopp

Strengths: Skilled marksman, inventive with gadgets.

Weaknesses: Tends to be cowardly, lacks physical strength.

Best Moves: Sniper Techniques, Usopp's Special Attacks.

Sanji

Strengths: Expert cook, powerful leg-based combatant.

Weaknesses: Has a soft spot for women, can be distracted easily.

Best Moves: Diable Jambe, Sky Walk.

Tony Tony Chopper

Strengths: Versatile with his Devil Fruit powers, skilled doctor.

Weaknesses: Often seen as naive, relies heavily on his transformations.

Best Moves: Monster Point, Heavy Point.

Nico Robin

Strengths: Highly intelligent, powerful Devil Fruit abilities.

Weaknesses: Vulnerable when using Devil Fruit powers.

Best Moves: Hana Hana no Mi Techniques, Clutch and Extend.

Franky

Strengths: Strong cyborg body, skilled shipwright.

Weaknesses: Over-reliance on his mechanical enhancements.

Best Moves: Franky Shogun, Coup de Vent.

Brook

Strengths: Immortal skeleton, skilled swordsman.

Weaknesses: Physical weakness from being a skeleton.

Best Moves: Soul Solid, The Sword of the Ice and Fire.

Shanks

Strengths: Powerful Haki, influential in the pirate world.

Weaknesses: Rarely fights, relies on his reputation.

Best Moves: Conqueror's Haki, Swordsmanship.

Whitebeard

Strengths: Tremendous physical strength, Earthquake powers.

Weaknesses: Illness, old age affecting his physical abilities.

Best Moves: Gura Gura no Mi Techniques, Tremor Tactics.

Monkey D. Garp

Strengths: Legendary Marine, formidable physical combat skills.

Weaknesses: His strong sense of justice sometimes clouds his judgment.

Best Moves: Haki Techniques, Powerful Punches.
